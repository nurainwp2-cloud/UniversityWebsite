STUDY AREAS

Explore Our Study Areas

Computing & Technology

The Computing & Technology programmes at APU is a part of the university's oldest and most established school. The school has a strong presence in the industry and is an obvious choice among the school leavers. APU offers a wide variety of specialised programmes that are very much industry driven and relevant; over the years, we have produced global citizens and industry-ready professionals.

Explore more

Engineering

APU's Engineering Programmes are current in terms of technology and are market driven, and thus have great employment opportunities. Apart from technical knowledge, the programme modules enable students to build leadership skills, communication skills and these skills prepare them for the workplace as professional and confident engineers. APU Engineering Degrees are fully accredited by the Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM) which is a signatory to the Washington Accord.

Explore more

Business & Management

In an era of global transformation, the demands placed upon business leaders to provide leadership and insights have become imperative. The Business & Management programmes at APU prepare students for a role that gives them an industry-relevant edge.

Explore more

Accounting & Finance

The Accounting and Finance programmes at APU are excellent springboards for students to launch their professional careers in accountancy or finance or to pursue further studies. They are well established, and are designed to provide a thorough understanding of the principles and practices of accounting, finance and related subjects. These programmes are well-accredited by professional bodies such as ACCA, CIMA, CPA, CTIM, ICAEW and more.

Explore more

Banking & Finance

With the help of Technology, embark onto a unique experience with APU’s Banking & Finance programmes. The unique aspect of APU's Banking & Finance programmes is that all programmes include modules of FinTech, where you will learn about key concepts on blockchain technology, robo advisory, cryptocurrencies and more.

Explore more

Design & Creative Media

Design lies behind everything we buy, use and desire. Good design is attractive, useable, practical and marketable. Good designers combine aesthetics with functionality, communications with entrepreneurialism, visual communications capability with effective written and spoken communications and the ability to work and empathise with others. Students of the APU Design & Creative Media programmes will be exposed to hands-on practical learning experiences in developing visuals and graphics that communicate messages well.

Explore more

Digital Marketing & Media

APU’s Digital Marketing and Media & Communication programmes are designed to prepare students for impactful careers in today’s digital and creative industries 

With exposure to areas like consumer behaviour, marketing communications, and strategic knowledge in digital tools, data analytics, social media, and online branding, students develop a deep understanding of how marketing impacts businesses globally. APU is also an approved partner of the Digital Marketing Institute (DMI), and students may opt for a CMI Level 5 Certificate in Management & Leadership through our APU-CMI Dual Accreditation pathway. 

The Media & Communication programme develops creative, analytical, and communication skills through practical learning and industry insights. Students may also opt for the CMI Level 5 Award to strengthen their leadership credentials in the media field.

Explore more

Actuarial Studies

APU’s Actuarial Studies programme is widely recognised by the Society of Actuaries (SOA), US and Institute of Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA), UK. Be a part of the fast-growing Actuarial industry with us; APU is also listed amongst the top Asian universities to receive maximum block exemptions at IFoA.

Explore more

Hospitality & Tourism

APU’s Hospitality & Tourism programme equips students with practical skills and global insights to thrive in one of the world’s fastest-growing industries. The curriculum blends hands-on learning with management theory, covering key areas from operations to strategic planning. Students graduate with the ability to lead in international hospitality and tourism environments. Through our Dual Award partnership with HTMi Switzerland, students in selected Hospitality & Tourism programme may receive two degree certificates—one from APU and one from HTMi—enhancing global recognition.

Explore more

International Relations

International Relations is an exciting and important discipline of study. It addresses key issues associated with the way countries of the world co-exist in the face of rapid change. We are seeing huge technological changes that affect conflicts, communications, trade and culture. There is a mass movement of people and a growing sophistication of global economic networks, while the world is politically and territorially divided into states there are great inequalities between them. Through the international learning experience at APU, students will become more prepared to become global leaders by interacting with students who come from different cultural backgrounds, as we promote cultural sensitivity and cultural exchange within our multinational students' community.

Explore more

Game Development

Game On! Contribute to the growth of the lucrative e-sports and computer games industry with skills that you will learn throughout your programme. Some of our notable alumni have achieved great heights in their careers and have become prominent figures in the industry, whether it is locally or internationally.

Explore more

Psychology

As the modern society gives more emphasis on mental health and psychological wellbeing, it is time for you to take up a career in Psychology. APU’s Psychology programme is infused with technology elements, which equips you with a stronger edge in the modern world.

Explore more

Education

The Master of Education in Learning Design and Technology at APU is an innovative programme designed to equip professionals with advanced skills and knowledge to address the learning needs in the rapidly evolving field of digital learning and instructional technology. The curriculum integrates the latest advancements in AI-driven learning environment, learning design, and creative assessment strategies, ensuring that students are prepared to address contemporary educational challenges. Through a series of hands-on projects and immersive learning experiences, students develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a mastery of learning design tools and techniques.

Architecture

This three-year Architecture degree programme is designed to equip aspiring architects with the skills to shape the future of the built environment. Focusing on digital technologies, sustainability, and architectural identity, the curriculum combines a strong architectural foundation with specialised courses addressing pressing global challenges.

Students will explore and innovate future architecture through technologies, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Virtual Reality (VR), and Building Information Modeling (BIM), while mastering hand sketching and drawing like master architects. This programme emphasises innovation, sustainability, and architectural identity, preparing graduates to lead the creation of smarter, more sustainable cities and communities while preserving the built environment quality and architectural identity.
